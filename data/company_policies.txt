Company Policy: Paid Leave

All full-time employees are entitled to 15 working days of paid leave per year.
Leave requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance.
Unused leave days can be carried over to the following year, up to a maximum of 5 days.
In case of medical emergencies, leave requests can be submitted with shorter notice, accompanied by proper documentation.
Paid leave is not cumulative beyond the carryover limit and resets every January 1st.
Part-time employees are granted paid leave on a pro-rata basis.

Company Policy: Remote Work

Employees are allowed to work remotely up to 3 days per week. Approval from the team lead is required.
Remote work days must be scheduled and logged in the internal calendar system.
Employees must be available during core working hours (10:00 AM to 4:00 PM local time).
A stable internet connection and a quiet work environment are required when working remotely.
Employees must attend all mandatory virtual meetings while working from home.
Full remote work (5 days per week) may be approved in special circumstances (e.g. relocation, health reasons), subject to HR review.

Company Policy: Working Hours
The standard workweek is 40 hours, Monday to Friday, from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
Employees are entitled to a 1-hour unpaid lunch break.
Flexible start and end times may be arranged with prior approval from the team lead.
Overtime must be pre-approved and is compensated either with time off in lieu or according to local labor laws.
